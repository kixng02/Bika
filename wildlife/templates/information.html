Streamlining Human-Wildlife Conflict Claims in South Africa

The Problem

Human-wildlife conflict is a serious and growing challenge in South Africa. When wildlife damages crops, livestock, or property, affected communities often face a long, frustrating, and paper-heavy process just to file a compensation claim.

Claimants must visit offices in person, fill in manual forms, and wait weeks — sometimes months — with little visibility into where their claim stands. For rural and farming communities already under pressure, this delay compounds the hardship.

Manual, paper-based claim submissions
No real-time status tracking for claimants
Slow, opaque review process for administrators
Fragmented communication between parties
Our Solution

Bika is a digital claims management platform that replaces manual processes with a fast, transparent, and accessible online system — accessible from any device, anywhere.

Claimants can submit evidence-backed claims in minutes using a guided step-by-step form. Administrators gain a real-time dashboard with analytics to review, approve, or decline claims efficiently and fairly.

Digital claims submission with photo evidence
Real-time claim status tracking
Admin analytics dashboard for oversight
Built-in support and community forum

Platform Features
Interactive Map
View nature reserves and wildlife areas across South Africa to understand conflict hotspots.
Guided Claim Submission
A simple 3-step form lets claimants select damage type, attach photo evidence, and add details — in minutes.
Claim Tracking
Claimants can view all their submitted claims and see their current status — pending, approved, or declined.
Admin Analytics Dashboard
Administrators see live charts and gauges — claims by category, monthly trends, approval rates, and ticket resolution metrics.
Support Tickets
Users can raise support tickets at any time. Admins assign, respond, and resolve tickets through a dedicated queue.
Community Forum
A built-in forum allows users to share experiences, ask questions, and support one another in dealing with wildlife conflict.
Announcements
Admins broadcast important updates and policy changes directly to all users through the announcements system.
Secure & Role-Based
Strict role separation between clients and staff ensures users only see what is relevant to them, keeping data secure.
Photo Evidence
Claimants attach photographic evidence directly to their claim, giving administrators the context needed to make fair decisions.
